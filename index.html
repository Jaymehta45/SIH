Making Our Oceans Safer, Together.

OceanIQ is a community-driven platform for real-time ocean hazard reporting. We empower sailors, surfers, researchers, and maritime industries with the data they need to navigate the waters safely and responsibly.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, accessible platform for reporting and viewing ocean hazards, preventing incidents and protecting both lives and marine ecosystems.
